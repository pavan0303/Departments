Explore Bangalore

About Bangalore

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital of Karnataka and a major hub for technology in India. Famous for its pleasant weather, vibrant culture, and lush greenery, Bangalore is a perfect blend of modernity and tradition.

Top Attractions

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Bangalore Palace
  • ISKCON Temple
  • Cubbon Park
  • Commercial Street (Shopping)

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bangalore is from October to February when the weather is cool and pleasant, making it ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
